# Comprehensive Marketing Plan: Contractor in Charge
A tailored lead generation and brand expansion strategy targeting home service contractors (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and other trades).

---

## Executive Summary & Positioning
**Contractor in Charge (CIC)** serves as the ultimate back-office operational and financial growth engine for growing trade businesses. Our marketing framework is built around **reclaiming time, maximizing lead conversion (stopping the "leaky bucket"), and establishing total financial visibility.**

### Target Persona: "Overwhelmed Owen"
- **Profile**: Owner-operator of a trade business (HVAC, plumbing, electrical) doing between $500K and $1.5M in annual revenue.
- **Pain Points**: Working 70-hour weeks; missing customer calls while out in the field; stressed about late/unreconciled QuickBooks files; frustrated that CRM data doesn't match the bank accounts; burnt out from wearing too many hats (scheduler, tech, bookkeeper, marketer).
- **Core Desires**: Wants to transition from "technician" to "CEO," scale past $2M safely, and spend weekends with family instead of chasing paperwork.

## Campaign Execution & Tracking Strategy
To ensure maximum ROI on this marketing plan, the following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) must be implemented and monitored inside a custom marketing dashboard:

### 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to Track:
1. **Speed-to-Lead (S2L)**: Time elapsed between a web form submission/LSA lead and live outbound dial (Goal: **< 3 minutes**).
2. **Lead Booking Rate (LBR)**: Percentage of inbound calls and web leads successfully booked into the FSM calendar (Goal: **> 85%**).
3. **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Total digital marketing spend divided by the number of new signed trade clients (Goal: **<$400**).
4. **FSM-QuickBooks Reconciled Discrepancies**: Number of unmapped inventory items or booking variances caught by daily reconciliation (Goal: **0 discrepancies weekly**).
5. **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Average length of a contractor's retention multiplied by monthly retainer fee (Goal: **> 36 months**).

### 🛠️ Recommended Tech Stack:
- **Lead Tracking & Attribution**: Google Analytics 4 (GA4) integrated with **Netrics.ai** (currently installed on the company website).
- **Email Automation**: ActiveCampaign or HubSpot (integrated with FSM webhooks to trigger follow-up sequences automatically).
- **Outbound SMS & Chat**: OnePath AI lead management platform.
- **Reporting**: Looker Studio dashboard compiling Google Ads, Facebook Ads, QBO metrics, and booking ratios.
